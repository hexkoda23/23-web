23 Look Brand Information:
23 Look is a luxury fashion brand founded in London. We focus on minimalist designs, monochrome palettes, and high-quality sustainable materials.
Our mission is to provide timeless pieces that transcend seasonal trends.
All our products are ethically crafted in Portugal and Italy.
We believe in "Less is More".
The brand was established in 2023.
Our flagship store is located in Soho, London.
We offer worldwide shipping to over 100 countries.
Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase for unworn items.
