Fashion Trends 2024-2025:
Minimalism continues to dominate the high-end market, with a focus on "Quiet Luxury".
Oversized silhouettes are still popular, but with more tailored structures.
Sustainable fabrics like organic cotton, linen, and Tencel are in high demand.
Monochromatic outfits (wearing one color head-to-toe) are a key styling trick for a cohesive look.
Capsule wardrobes are becoming essential - having 30-40 versatile pieces that mix and match.
Streetwear is evolving into "Smart Streetwear" - combining hoodies/sneakers with blazers and tailored trousers.
Neutral tones: Black, White, Beige, Grey, and Navy are the foundational colors.
Statement accessories are used to elevate simple outfits.
