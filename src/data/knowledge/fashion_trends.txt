Q: What are the latest fashion trends (2024–2025)?
A: Quiet luxury, sharp tailoring, elevated basics, textured monochromes, and statement outerwear define the moment — think premium knits, leather, and crisp denim.

Q: How do I style oversized fits?
A: Balance volume with structure — pair an oversized jacket with tapered trousers or a boxy tee with tailored shorts. Add sleek sneakers or loafers to refine the silhouette.

Q: How do I build a capsule wardrobe?
A: Start with neutral tops, tailored bottoms, a versatile outer layer, premium denim, and clean footwear. Choose timeless cuts; rotate seasonal accents for freshness.

Q: How do I wear monochrome without looking flat?
A: Mix textures and tones within the same color family: matte vs. gloss, knit vs. leather, light vs. deep shades to create depth.

Q: What accessories elevate minimal fits?
A: Slim belts, structured caps, refined chains, and subtle watches — choose one hero piece per outfit to keep the look focused.
