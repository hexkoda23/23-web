Q: How do I wash cotton?
A: Machine wash cold (30°C) with similar colors; air dry or tumble low to preserve shape.

Q: How do I care for wool?
A: Hand wash with wool detergent or dry clean; never tumble dry. Lay flat to dry.

Q: How do I clean leather?
A: Spot clean with a damp cloth; condition every 6 months. Avoid soaking and heat.

Q: How do I care for denim?
A: Wash sparingly, inside-out, in cold water. Air dry to preserve color and fit.

Q: How do I care for silk?
A: Dry clean recommended. If hand washing, use silk-specific detergent in cold water; do not wring.

Q: How should I store knitwear?
A: Fold knits to avoid stretching; use padded hangers for structured jackets.

Q: How do I handle stains?
A: Treat immediately; blot, don’t rub. Test stain removers on an inconspicuous area first.
